UNRIVALLED EXPERTISE AND EXPERIENCE

A trusted OTR tyre procurement and dispersal partner of many of the world’s largest mining and civil construction operators, Jewell Tyres have over 45 years experience buying and selling mining and earthmover tyres. We are specialists in sourcing under-allocated and difficult to procure OTR tyres, and maximising returns on surplus tyre inventory liquidations.

Leveraging our unrivalled Australian and international networks and custom-built tyre availability database, we source mining and earthmover tyre stocks from manufacturers, wholesalers, retailers and OTR traders. Additionally, we have exclusive access to tyres from machinery dismantlers, auction houses, import & export agents, specialist tyre stockists, mines, quarries, civil contractors and a variety of other unique sources.

Proudly family owned and operated, Jewell Tyres have grown from humble beginnings in rural Victoria to become Australia’s leading independent mining and earthmover tyre trader. Our reputation has been built on service, reliability, back-up and support with a deep commitment to confidentiality and timely delivery.
